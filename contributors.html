Contributors

Heated City is a global directory where designers, architects, urban planners, and climate-focused companies worldwide have shared their visions for cities in 2030 and beyond. Our contributors are passionate about creating sustainable, resilient, and innovative urban environments that will transform the way we inhabit our world.
