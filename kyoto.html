Kyoto

Discover a curated selection of innovative and sustainable projects, blending Kyoto's rich heritage with forward-thinking practices.

Curated by Himari Satō

Hikari Machiya Studio (光町家スタジオ)

Local artisans offer workshops in traditional crafts such as indigo dyeing and washi paper making.

Akari no Michi (明かりの道)

Solar-powered lanterns line the streets, designed by local artists.

Hozugawa Eco-Island Center (保津川エコアイランドセンター)

Miniature ecosystems along the Hozugawa River.

Bamboo Grove Knowledge Trail (竹林知識トレイル)

IoT-enabled monitoring of carbon capture and soil health.

Tsubasa Wildlife Eco-Bridge (つばさ野生動物エコブリッジ)

Green bridges for animals, doubling as scenic walking trails.

Kura no Megumi (倉の恵み)

Traditional sake breweries powered by solar energy.

Midori no Kura (緑の蔵)

Its operations are powered by bioenergy from sake production waste.

Yume no Mizu (夢の水)

Guided tours showcasing renewable energy in sake brewing.
